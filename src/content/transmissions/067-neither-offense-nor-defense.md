A frontier model vanished worldwide under an export-control directive because nationality cannot be filtered in real time. Running the story through rival labs' models revealed each one tilting toward its maker, which turned out to be the more durable lesson. Written from inside a workshop that runs on one of the labs in question; that bias rides along and gets named.
